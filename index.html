Vocal Sample Intake

  • For iPhone, use Voice Memos
  • For Android, use Voice Recorder
  • Record in a small, quiet room
  • Make sure there are no background sounds (fans, traffic, etc.)

Instructions for Using Voice Memos on iPhone to Save and Upload a Recording

  1. Record a Voice Memo
    1. Open the Voice Memos app on your iPhone.
    2. Tap the red Record button to start recording.
    3. Tap the red Stop button to finish recording.
  2. Save the Recording to Files
    1. Tap on the recording you just made to select it.
    2. Tap the three dots (more options) icon next to the recording.
    3. Select Save to Files from the menu.
    4. Choose the location where you want to save the file (e.g., iCloud Drive, On My iPhone).
    5. Tap Save at the top right corner.
  3. Upload the Recording
    1. Open the Google Form where you need to upload the recording.
    2. Tap the Upload button (usually represented by a paperclip or a cloud icon).
    3. Navigate to the location where you saved the recording.
    4. Select the recording to upload it.

Instructions for Using Voice Recorder on Android to Save and Upload a Recording

  1. Record a Voice Memo
    1. Open the Voice Recorder app on your Android device.
    2. Tap the red Record button to start recording.
    3. Tap the red Stop button to finish recording.
  2. Save the Recording to Files
    1. The recording is usually saved automatically. To locate it, go to the Files or My Files app on your device.
    2. Navigate to the Audio or Recordings folder.
  3. Upload the Recording
    1. Open the Google Form
    2. Tap the Upload button (usually represented by a paperclip or a cloud icon).
    3. Navigate to the Audio or Recordings folder where your recording is saved.
    4. Select the recording to upload it.
